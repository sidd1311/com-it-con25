Com-IT-Con 2024

The International Conference on Progressive Computational Intelligence, Information Technology, and Networking (Com-IT-Con 2024) aims to serve as a premier platform for the exchange of knowledge, ideas, and innovations among researchers, academics, practitioners, and industry professionals in the dynamic fields of computational intelligence, information technology, and networking. By bringing together experts from diverse backgrounds and disciplines, the conference endeavors to facilitate interdisciplinary collaboration, foster research advancements, and address current challenges and emerging trends in these rapidly evolving domains. Through presentations of cutting-edge research, interactive discussions, and networking opportunities, Com-IT-Con 2024 strives to promote innovation, stimulate intellectual discourse, and contribute to the collective advancement of computational intelligence, information technology, and networking. Moreover, by providing a venue for the publication of high-quality research papers and offering educational resources such as workshops and tutorials, the conference seeks to nurture talent, disseminate knowledge, and inspire the next generation of scholars and practitioners in these critical fields.

Com-IT-Con 2023

The International Conference on Cloud and Parallel Computing, Artificial and Business Intelligence, Quantum and Machine Learning: Trends, Perspectives, and Prospects, Com-IT-Con 2023 was a significant gathering of experts, policymakers, academics, and industry leaders from around the world. Held in Block A, School of Engineering & Technology, MRIIRS, the conference aimed to promote knowledge exchange, foster collaborations, and explore innovative solutions to address pressing global challenges in sustainability and development. Com-IT-Con is the annual International Conference Series of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the School of Engineering and Technology, MRIIRS, Faridabad, Haryana.  The proceedings of the Com-IT-Con 2023 are available at:

COM-IT-CON 2022

​

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering, FET, Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies (MRIIRS) has organized the two-day International Conference on "Machine Learning, Big Data, Cloud and Parallel Computing: Trends, Perspectives and Prospects" (COM-IT-CON-2022), co-sponsored by IEEE Delhi Section. The main objective of this conference is to bring together academicians, researchers, and industry leaders from around the world to discuss and debate the most recent advancements in computing disciplines like Machine Learning, Big Data, Cloud Computing, and Parallel Computing. The conference aims to establish scientifically the link between research team and experts from other nations to facilitate future collaboration. In this two-day event, we will see presentations in different tracks such as Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Cloud Computing & Virtualization, Big Data Analytics & Virtualization, Semantic Web & Information Retrieval, Blockchain & Cyber Security, Data Science & Engineering, GPU & Parallel Computing. The Conference aims to provide an opportunity to share the ideas, build competency, contribute to advancement using technology and tools and learn about new offerings in different domains.

The proceedings of the Com-IT-Con 2022 are available at

https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/xpl/conhome/9850394/proceeding
