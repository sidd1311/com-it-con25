Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies

Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies (MRIIRS), formerly MRIU, a Deemed-to-be-University under section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956, and a NAAC accredited ‘A++’ , ISO certified, UGC Category-I Institution, is a continuum of excellence from the Career Institute of Technology and Management (CITM). MRIIRS has been awarded a 5-Star QS rating for Teaching, Facilities & Social Responsibility. MRIIRS is ranked among top institutions by the Ministry of Education’s NIRF Ranking. It strives for high-quality education in the fields of Engineering & Technology, Health & Applied Sciences, Management, Computer Applications, Hotel Management, Commerce, Humanities, Media, Architecture, Design, Liberal Arts, Economics, Applied Psychology and many more. MRIIRS is a Founder Member of the prestigious “College Board’s Indian Global Higher Education Alliance”.


Department of Computer Science and Engineering 

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was established in 1997 with a focus on imparting the fundamental concepts both theoretically and practically. Keeping in view the growth in industry and increasing demand for computer professionals, the department also offers some industry associated courses. To meet the needs of industry and R&D organizations, the course curriculum has been carefully customized to meet the rising global outlook and focuses on forthcoming innovations in the field of Computer Science and Engineering. Computer Science and Engineering is a highly sought-after course because it acts as a catalyst and allows one to keep up with the digital era. The platforms are incredibly diversified, with a huge range of job opportunities. This course is offered to students at the undergraduate as well as the postgraduate levels and students obtain the B.Tech and M.Tech degrees, respectively. The B.Tech (CSE) programme offered by the Department has been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) in 2003, 2007, 2018 and 2023. ​


Com-IT-Con 2025

The International Conference on Progressive Computational Intelligence, Information Technology, and Networking (Com-IT-Con 2025) aims to serve as a premier platform for the exchange of knowledge, ideas, and innovations among researchers, academics, practitioners, and industry professionals in the dynamic fields of computational intelligence, information technology, and networking. By bringing together experts from diverse backgrounds and disciplines, the conference endeavors to facilitate interdisciplinary collaboration, foster research advancements, and address current challenges and emerging trends in these rapidly evolving domains. Through presentations of cutting-edge research, interactive discussions, and networking opportunities, Com-IT-Con 2025 strives to promote innovation, stimulate intellectual discourse, and contribute to the collective advancement of computational intelligence, information technology, and networking. Moreover, by providing a venue for the publication of high-quality research papers and offering educational resources such as workshops and tutorials, the conference seeks to nurture talent, disseminate knowledge, and inspire the next generation of scholars and practitioners in these critical fields.
